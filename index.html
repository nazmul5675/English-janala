Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes! You can access most of our lessons and exercises for free. Some advanced courses may require a subscription.
3. Do I need to create an account?
Creating an account is optional, but it allows you to track your progress, save your exercises, and access personalized recommendations.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by using flashcards, reading articles, practicing with quizzes, and engaging in our daily word challenges.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes! After completing a course, you can receive a certificate of completion that you can download or share on LinkedIn.
